Bungō Stray Dogs

Servamp

Servamp (SERVAMP -サーヴァンプ)s a Japanese manga series by Strike Tanaka, serialized in Media Factory's shōjo manga magazine Monthly Comic Gene since 2011. A television anime adaptation has been announced and will begin airing in July 2016.
